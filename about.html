✦ Our Story

About Wanderlust Voyages

More than a travel agency — we are your trusted guide to the world's most remarkable places.

✦ Who We Are

Born from a Love of Travel

Wanderlust Voyages was founded in 2012 by two passionate travellers, Amara Dube and Luca Ferretti, who believed that travel should be more than just a holiday — it should be a life-changing experience.


From our humble beginnings as a boutique Cape Town agency with three staff members and a single brochure, we have grown into a nationally recognised travel brand serving thousands of South African adventurers every year.


Our Mission: To connect people with the world's most extraordinary destinations through carefully crafted, personalised travel experiences that create memories lasting a lifetime.
